The missing piece
THE
IMPLEMENTOR
Marketwake has the ideas, the strategy, the craft, the coaching, and the creative vision. The one archetype not yet fully present is the Implementor - the person whose entire job is to take the decisions made by the Visionary, Innovator, and Strategist and build them systematically into the company across all teams.

Without this role, great ideas stay in conversations. With it, everything the team described in their vision responses becomes organizational reality. This is the hire that makes One House possible.
