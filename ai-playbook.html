Which tools Claude
replaces, reduces, or enhances.

Based on your 2026 tools audit, here is every tool assessed against what Claude can now do. Some are redundant and should be cancelled. Some can be reduced. Some get dramatically more powerful with Claude layered on top.

Cancel — Claude replaces
These tools can be fully replaced by Claude workflows
Qwilr
$1,680/yr · Kay/Megan
Why cancel
Qwilr is a delivery wrapper. Claude generates the full proposal and SOW content from discovery notes — scope, deliverables, pricing rationale, assumptions. The output lives in Google Docs or a branded template. The content generation is 100% Claude-replaceable.
Replace with
Claude proposal prompt → Google Doc template with Marketwake branding. Already in the AI Playbook as Workflow 01.
ChatGPT
~$5,640/yr · Natalie

Claude handles everything ChatGPT does for content writing, strategy, research, and drafting — and is better suited for the long-form professional work Marketwake produces. Paying for both is redundant overhead.
Replace with

Rev (Transcription)
Variable · Kristen
Why cancel
Claude transcribes, summarizes, and extracts action items from recordings in one step. Rev charges per minute for a single output. Claude produces the transcript plus the summary plus the next steps simultaneously.
Replace with

$1,943/yr · Megan
